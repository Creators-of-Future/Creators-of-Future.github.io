Creators of Future

The future belongs to the generalists. The more tools you have in your arsenal, the better you'll navigate the uncertainty of the rapidly changing world.

What's Creators of Future?

Creators of Future is a mission to help more people become generalists. I think being a designer with development skills or vice-versa is underrated. The ability to create usable and delightful stuff out of nothing is magical. It's a super-power. Add marketing and sales, and you're unstoppable.

The first batch of this program is for people who: want to build iOS apps; already have an idea; are either a designer or a developer. The mentorship will involve one-on-one guidance on UI/UX or SwiftUI, depending on your existing skillset.

If you belong to the above cohort, reach out via the form. I'll select up to 5 applications, beginning April 1st, 2021, who will also be the first members of the Creators of Future Tribe. I'll help you in the journey of publishing your app to the App Store from start to finish. (If you already have an app published and are looking to improve your design skills, feel free to apply as well).

The applications for this cohort are now closed. Sign up via the form to subscribe to updates.

How Much Does it Cost?

This is a free, no-strings-attached mentorship program. The long-term goal of this program is to create a strong network of generalists and founders.

If you, however, strongly believe in the mission and want to support it, you can sponsor via Patreon or donate via BTC or ETH.

About Me

Hi, I am Kushagra Agarwal. I started my career in 2010, and I've been a generalist throughout—mostly wearing the hat of a designer, but also helping with development on the side.

I've also worked on several side-projects, most notably, CSSBattle, Cone, and Unwind. Apart from design and development, side projects have also taught me a bunch about marketing, sales, and growth.

Through this mentorship, I aim to share all that I've learned throughout my career and help you become a generalist and a future creator.

If you have any questions, suggestions, concerns, or if you are a generalist who would like to be a part of this mission, please reach out. Let's build the future together. ✌
